Scan your disposal

Unlock the power of smart recycling with our 'Scan Your Disposals' feature. By simply taking a photo of an item, our app instantly identifies the material and provides clear, step-by-step instructions on how to recycle it properly. This innovative technology ensures that every piece of waste is disposed of correctly, making recycling easier and more efficient than ever before. Embrace the convenience of scanning your disposals and contribute to a cleaner, greener planet

Collect points

Turn your recycling efforts into rewards with our 'Collect Points' feature. Each time you properly dispose of recyclable materials using our app, you'll earn points that can be redeemed for various eco-friendly rewards and incentives. This gamified approach not only makes recycling fun but also motivates you to stay committed to your sustainability goals. Start collecting points today and enjoy the satisfaction of contributing to a greener planet while earning rewards for your eco-conscious actions.
